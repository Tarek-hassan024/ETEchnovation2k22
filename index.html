Events In The ETEchnovation 2K22

Line Follower Racing

The Line follower robot is an automated vehicle that follows a visual line embedded on the surface. This visual line is a path on which the line follower robot runs. Generally, it uses a black line on a white surface, or you can adjust it as a white line on a black surface

Project Showcase

You are invited to submit a proposal to participate in our Project Showcase. The Showcase is an informal exhibition of real-world projects from around 35 discipline. This is a great way to show off your work to the community of UX professionals.

Poster Presentation

A poster presentation, at a congress or conference with an academic or professional focus, is the presentation of research information in the form of a paper poster that conference participants may view. A poster session is an event at which many such posters are presented.

Gaming Contest

Playing games help us grow, think, and find solutions to problems. The Gaming Contest calls for all entries about playing games and projects that pay tribute to your favorite games. Video game guides, easter egg tips, tabletop games, and tribute projects — if it’s inspired by a game you love to play, it's eligible for this contest!
